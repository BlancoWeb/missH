Welcome to Learning with Miss H

A website offering parents, carers and teachers creative and engaging ideas and resources to support and inspire learning with their pupils, whether it is in a school environment or in a home setting. Resources on this website are aimed for ages up to age 12.

Having taught within schools for over a decade, I want to share my knowledge, ideas and expertise when it comes to providing worthwhile, stimulating lessons and activities. I have a passion for creating teaching resources and fun, hands-on activities linked to all areas of the curriculum.

If you take the time to browse through my website, you will see what resources I have available. They range from Cross-curricular topic plans to activities supporting subject areas. Most resources can be purchased (at a small fee), however there are lots of beneficial free resources too.

Thank you very much for taking the time to visit my website.
